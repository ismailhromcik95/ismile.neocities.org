iSmile Dec 22, 2025

iSmile's Discord Rules:

1. Refrain from profanities (keep it family-friendly).

2. Refrain from bringing politics, ideologies into discussion, since this is supposed to only be a fun, light-hearted server.

3. Respect other people's privacy.

4. Make sure you're posting in the correct channel.

5. Do not spam outside the channels dedicated to spamming.

6. Do not promote gambling, alcohol, smoking, drugs or any other destructive activity.

7. No offensive names or profile pictures.

You do not have permission to send messages in this channel.
